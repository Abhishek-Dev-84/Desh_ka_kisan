Key Features of Desh Ka Kisan

🛒 Direct Buyer-Seller Connect

Farmers can list their crops and sell directly to buyers without any middlemen. This increases transparency and ensures better pricing for both parties.

📍 Local Market Listings

Farmers and buyers can view real-time listings relevant to their local region, enabling smoother trade and quicker delivery of fresh produce.

📦 Easy Product Catalog

Farmers can upload images, details, prices, and availability of their products in a simple and organized format. Buyers can view, compare, and contact directly.

📱 Role-Based Login

Separate login flows for buyers and sellers. Farmers get access to listing tools, while buyers can view catalogs, place interest, or chat with sellers.

🌐 Multi-Language Interface

Switch between English and Hindi for a personalized experience. Helps farmers who are more comfortable in their native language.

🔒 Secure and Verified Accounts

Only verified users can list or contact others. This ensures a safe and trustworthy platform for all users, especially first-time digital farmers.
